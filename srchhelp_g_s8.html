Government Document Call Number Search

Type the SuDoc or MoDoc Document Number:

Type as much of the SuDoc or MoDoc Document Number as you know.

SuDoc: Superintendent of Documents number used for United States government documents; MoDoc: call number used for Missouri State government documents.

for example

  • SuDoc Numbers
    • C 3.186/3-2:992
    • D 101.22:550-25/990
    • HE 20.3152:F 94/4
    • LC 33.10:
  • MoDoc Numbers
    • MO ECON To 12:9996-97
    • MO ED 4:sch 1/
    • MO NR.En 2:
    • MO PS.Hp 5:In 7/

search tips

  • Always type MO at the beginning of a MoDoc number.
